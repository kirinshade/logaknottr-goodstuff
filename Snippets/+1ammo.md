This snippet adds damage and to-hit bonuses to attack rolls. It also tracks ammo expenditures via custom counters, fully supporting the `-rr` argument.

Supported Custom Counter Names:
* +1 Arrows
* +1 Blowgun Needles
* +1 Crossbow Bolts
* +1 Energy Cells
* +1 Modern Bullets
* +1 Renaissance Bullets
* +1 Sling Bullets

Supported weapon names (case insensitive):

Futuristic:
antimatter rifle, laser pistol, laser rifle

Modern:
automatic pistol, automatic rifle, hunting rifle, revolver, shotgun

Renaissance:
pistol, musket

Slings:
sling, hoopak

Fantasy:
blowgun, crossbow, longbow, shortbow, bow
