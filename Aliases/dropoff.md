This alias is used to "drop off" goods and receive payment using the following formulae:
* Gold = items / 10
* Gold Leaf Coins = items / 100 (rounded down)

No tradeoffs are made between the currencies; they are both received simultaneously.
Only up to three Gold Leaf Coins are awarded for any given transaction

This alias does not take arguments, and exclusively is used in conjunction with [eternalphoenix64's Survival collection](https://avrae.io/dashboard/workshop/612919be807f1f1578ee9ccf).
When using that collection, do not assign a custom location for your bags. This alias will not work!
